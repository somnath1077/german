There are two types of adjective endings based on whether a definite 
article is used or not. If an indefinite article is used (ein, eine, ...)
the ending is called a strong ending; if a definite article is used, the 
ending is called a weak ending. 

STRONG ENDINGS: with indefinite articles
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
           M.      F.      N.      Pl.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Nom.       er      e       es      e                                      
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Akk.       en      e       es      e                                      
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Dat.       en      en      en      en                                     
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Gen.       en      en      en      er                                     
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

WEAK ENDINGS: with definite articles
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
           M.      F.      N.      Pl.                                     
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Nom.       e       e       e       en                                      
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Akk.       en      e       e       en                                      
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Dat.       en      en      en      en                                      
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Gen.       en      en      en      en                                      
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
